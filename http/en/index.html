Aymon

Aymon, ‘late & dry hopped’, reddish-gold beer with mild malty character, outstanding flavour of whole hops and aromatic bitter taste.

Bayard Steed beer

Brewed in honor of Duke Aymon, lord of Dendermonde and the legend of the Bayard Steed ridden by the Four Sons of Aymon.

Aymon beer was brewed by Ros Beiaard Bierproevers for the first time in the year 2000 for the Bayard Steed parade. The most recent year of brewing is 2023.

info@aymon.be

Beer to share

Aymon parades in 75cl bottles, it's beer to share.

Alc. 8% vol. IBU: 35. EBC: 25.

World Beer Awards

Aymon received the 'Country Winner' award at the World Beer Awards 2020. The jury especially appreciated the floral and cider aromas. Aymon is light and easy to drink, they added.
